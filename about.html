Group Profile

Demographic Information

Our group is made up of three members, all from Hanoi, Vietnam. Despite sharing the same hometown, each of us brings unique perspectives and experiences to the group. Two of us are 20 years old, and one is 27 years old, which allows us to have a mix of youthful energy and more experienced viewpoints.

Growing up in Hanoi, we’ve seen how technology has rapidly developed in our city, and this has fueled our passion for web development and programming. We all share a common goal of using our skills to contribute to the tech industry, both locally and globally. Our different ages and experiences help us collaborate effectively, combining fresh ideas with practical insights.

As a team, we draw on these shared experiences to approach challenges with creativity and a strong sense of community.
